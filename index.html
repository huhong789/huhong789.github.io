Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at the Pennsylvania State University. My research interests include system and software security. I like to work on vulnerability detection, exploitation and defense.

I received my PhD degree from National University of Singapore in 2016 working with Prof. Zhenkai Liang, and obtained my B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2011. I spent three years in Georgia Institute Technology as a postdoctoral fellow and research scientist, working with Prof. Wenke Lee and Taesoo Kim.
